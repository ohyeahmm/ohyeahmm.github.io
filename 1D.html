THINKING BOX

Domains

physical computing, praxiology(human behavior)

Concept

Awake (觉醒)

a·wake

əˈwāk/

verb

make people to awake to awarness to think. The idea of awake, it was influenced by life and also by certain sci-fi movies. Parpika,Perfect Blue, The Matrix and Blade Runner. I'm very into the action of people to start to awake. During my mind research, a ted talk by Oliver Sacks, Neurologist, about what hallucination reveals about our minds. Oliver brings the attention that when visually impaired people experience lucid hallucinations. Which is also called people's imagination. So I decided to use physical computing and human behavior to create a project base on using imagination and interaction to make this awake project can be come true.

Story

In 2045, the whole world has entered the era of technology. People’s lives have been completely taken over by artificial intelligence. Everything looks perfect and looks like a harmonious and advanced society. However, this world is controlled by a group of people, no one knows who they are. People don’t realize that their lives are well planned by these group of people. Until his appearance…

It’s a normal Friday night, Evan is on the way home, suddenly a man with all white suit and wearing a blue sunglass walking towards to him and talked to him with a very deep voice, “It’s time to be awake”. Then he was gone, and left Evan with very confused, Evan doesn’t realize what just happened. He feels very inexplicable, he picked up his food in the store, still the same thing strawberry milk and cheese cake. He he came out from the store and leaned on side of the street to prepare to open his food, he saw a strange logo on the ground. He felt a little strange and planned to take it home to eat. Who is that guy? Evan stares at the fish tank, then start to eat his food.

A week past…

When Evan back to home he felt someone is in his house. He almost trigger the crisis button, the guy start to talk to him, “I won’t hurt you, I’m here to ask you to join me”. Evan asked: “Join you for what? Why me?” The man laughed “Hahah, I know you will curious why I chose you, because your father was my best partner. ” Evan was shocked, his father, no one mentioned about his parents before. Because he was grow up in orphanage. The man start to talking again: “I know this is to much information for you I’m sorry. The reason I’m here to find you because this world is crazy, it is not as peaceful as people think as. This world was controlled by 10 large families, they are evil.” Evan asked: “Why? Why they are evil?” The man answered :” because Artificial intelligence has become the brain of people, and people’s bodies are just a body. ” Evan : “What do you need me for?” The man: “ I need your help to build this thinking machine for people. This idea was actually your father’s idea, but he and your mother was killed when you were 4 years old, because those people found out what he wants to do, they killed them. I’m sorry to let you know this. I was the person sent you to the orphanage, before they find you. I’m sorry Evan. ” Evan was shocked and angry, his parents, he has parents. But he forgot their look he was mad at himself. Quickly Evan adjusted his emotions. What is this thinking machine again? Evan asked again. The man: “ Thinking machine is a toy that adults and children both could play with it. At the time when they start to play the game they will start to think again they will start to awareness their life and their surroundings and this world, but we need to make sure their AI steward won’t notice it. People gonna wake up gradually, even themselves won’t notice it.” Evan got so many questions in his mind. “How is it gonna work?” The man firm answer to : “Remember we see with the eyes, but we see with brain as well, and seeing with the brain is often called imagination. hallucination reveals about our minds. We need to make people to use their imagination to awake themselves. What we need to do we just need to build this “toy” and sell it to this world. And use human behavior to make them to awake.”
